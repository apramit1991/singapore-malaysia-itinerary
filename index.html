Day 3 - Monday, Aug 11
🏖️ Sentosa & Beaches
SG$49-77
Morning: Take MRT to HarbourFront and walk the Sentosa Boardwalk (free) or ride Sentosa Express (SG$4). Spend morning at Siloso Beach or Palawan Beach - swim, relax and take photos on the suspension bridge to Southernmost Point of Continental Asia (free).
Afternoon: For panoramic views, ride the Mount Faber-Sentosa Cable Car (SG$24.50-35) or hike the Imbiah Nature Trail or visit Fort Siloso Skywalk (free).
Evening: Watch the Wings of Time multimedia show at the beach (SG$17.10).

Day 6 - Thursday, Aug 14
🇲🇾 Kuala Lumpur (Day 2)
~RM 100 (~SG$30)
Morning: Take KTM train or Grab to Batu Caves. The main temple cave is free to enter; transport costs about RM 1.30 each way by train or RM 3-4 by Grab. Climb the colorful 272 steps for epic photos.
Afternoon: Visit Kuala Lumpur Forest Eco Park (canopy walk) - small entrance fee (~RM 10). Explore historic Merdeka Square, Sultan Abdul Samad Building, Thean Hou Temple and Central Market (free).
Evening: Enjoy dinner in Chinatown.
Optional: Side-trip to Malacca - 2hr bus (RM 10-14 each way) to explore Jonker Street Night Market and A Famosa ruins.

📸 Instagram-Worthy Photo Tips

🌅 Golden Hour: Plan visits to Supertree Grove, Marina Bay and Helix Bridge in the evening for vibrant skies with soft light.
🌺 Gardens by the Bay: The Cloud Forest waterfall and aerial walkways make dramatic backdrops. Arrive early or late to avoid crowds.
🎨 Haji Lane & Kampong Glam: Colorful murals, shophouses and the Sultan Mosque are perfect for street-style photos.
🏖️ Sentosa Beaches: The suspension bridge at Palawan Beach and WWII-era Fort Siloso Skywalk offer unique angles.
🏙️ Marina Bay Sands SkyPark: Though not cheap, the observation deck gives panoramic shots of the skyline.
🇲🇾 Kuala Lumpur: The vibrant steps of Batu Caves, mirrored Petronas towers, and KL Forest Eco Park canopy walk provide eye-catching scenes.
🌿 Nature Spots: MacRitchie Reservoir treetop walk and Pulau Ubin offer lush greenery and wildlife; wide-angle lens captures depth.
